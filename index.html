ABOUT BIGG ROCK

BIGG ROCK Excavating was founded in 2012 as a land development contractor for new construction and underground pipe repair and maintenance. New land development consists of four primary areas: rough cutting of roads and grading, underground trenching for water, sewer, storm drain and telephone line.

BIGG ROCK has focused primarily on new land development along the Wasatch Front region of Utah. BIGG ROCK also works directly with municipalities to perform pipe installation, repair and maintenance.
